W.H. Hoopes 18 November 1840–17 April 1901

Hoopes came to Muscatine in 1854 and became the most prominent produce grower and seller of Muscatine Island. Hoopes helped start the Heinz plant in Muscatine and acquired 2,500 acres of land on the island.
